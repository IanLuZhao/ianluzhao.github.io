About Me

Hi, I'm Yuang Lu-Zhao, a high school senior passionate about technology and innovation. I'm interested in computer science, robotics, and machine learning. I aim to pursue a degree in Computer Science and contribute to impactful tech projects. My inspiration for CS stems from my love for video games, which developed at a young age. Like many other teenagers, I spend lots of my spare time entertaining myself through video games and YouTube videos. This led me to attempt coding and eventually try and make my own game. Through this experience, I've discovered that I love to see my ideas flourish and come to life. I hope that I can make an impact in the future, helping others and creating a more relaxing, enjoyable world.
